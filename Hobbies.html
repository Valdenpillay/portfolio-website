My 1989 Toyota Corolla Rebuild

Undertook a comprehensive restoration project in memory of my grandfather, focusing on painting, panel beating, and the repair of both mechanical and electrical components of this timeless classic. The project included a full respray, upgraded rims, enhanced sound system, and complete mechanical refurbishment, covering brakes, suspension, chassis, gearbox, and clutch. Additionally, all auto electrical systems were restored and modernized, featuring the installation of LED daytime running lights and underbody lighting.
